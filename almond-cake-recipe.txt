Ingredients:
 - 1/4 cup sugar
 - 1 cup almond flour
 - two eggs
 - 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
 - 

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour one 6-inch round baking pan. Put parchment paper on the bottom.
 
 2. Separate the eggs. Whip the whites in a kitchenaid type mixer. 

 3. In a separate bowl, stir the sugar into the egg yolks.
 
 4. Stir the almond flour into the egg yolk mixture.

 5. Gently fold the whipped whites into the egg yolk mixture.

 6. Pour into prepared pan.

 7. Bake 40 minutes.

 8. Cool & remove carefully.
